Students making choices

This project is a perspective that shows you a life of a student. But you have the control to make decisions and choose your ending. There are good and bad endings. They go from a small scale of good/bad to a big scale good/bad. I thought about this project because I feel like many student's aren't really taking school seriously but that's all up to them but I want them to acknowledge that those bad habits are a loop that you can stop. More like a reflection. This will be easy for the good kiddos that know exactly what they're doing! Theese choices are supposed to define your future but I view it more as of the choices you create now for example good choices can help create a more high percentage of success in your future as students who think about it now. Time goes fast!

I wish I could've add a percentage of success that you would have at the end if you kept going with the bad habit or good habit in the future.
